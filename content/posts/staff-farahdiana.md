## Biography

Farahdiana Wan Yunus obtained her PhD in Microengineering and Nanoelectronics from the National University of Malaysia in 2021, Master of Science in Photonics and Device Microfabrication from the University of Strathclyde, UK in 2015, and Bachelor of Science in Industrial Physics from University Technology MARA (UiTM), Malaysia in 2014. She pursued a Postdoctoral Fellowship at the National Institute for Material Science (NIMS), Japan, and is now working as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Physics, Xiamen University Malaysia. Her research focuses on MEMS device fabrications and applications.

## Research Interests

**MEMS Fabrication:** MEMS fabrication processes including photolithography, wet etching, deposition of electrodes, lift-off processes, electrochemical etching for silicon nanopores (< 50 nm), and characterization of silicon membranes using SEM and particle size analysis.

**MEMS Applications:** Cell separation using dielectrophoresis, silicon membranes for biological samples, polymer coating on silicon for biocompatibility, humidity sensors for organic compounds selectivity.
