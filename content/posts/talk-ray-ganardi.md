## Research Focus

Dr. Ray Ganardi's work focuses on characterising the **state transformation laws** for various quantum resource theories. Quantum resource theories provide a rigorous framework for understanding which quantum states can be converted into one another under physically motivated constraints — and quantifying the resources required or produced in such transformations.

## About the Speaker

Dr. Ray Ganardi received his BSc (Hons) in Physics from **Nanyang Technological University**, and later obtained his PhD from the **University of Gdańsk**. He is currently a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Warsaw, where his research focuses on the mathematical structure of quantum resources and their transformation laws.
