## Biography

Prof. Ong is the Head of the Physics Department. He is also an Adjunct Professor at the National University of Singapore and a Visiting Professor at Lanzhou University, China. He is a Fellow of the Institute of Physics, UK.

He served on the editorial boards of *Superconductor Science and Technology* (2002–2004), *International Journal of Modern Physics B* (2002–2006), and *Modern Physics Letter B* (2002–2006). He was a frequent visitor to the International Centre for Theoretical Physics (ICTP), Trieste, Italy, and Atomic Energy Research Establishment, Harwell, UK.

He has published more than 600 papers with an h-index of 50, authored one book on microwave published by Wiley, and holds a US patent. He supervised 34 PhD students, 34 MSc students and 58 Honours students during his time at the National University of Singapore. He was awarded the University Outstanding Researcher Award at the National University of Singapore in 2010.
