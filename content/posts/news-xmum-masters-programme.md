*Published on July 7, 2025*

Xiamen University Malaysia (XMUM) has introduced a new **Master's Programme in Semiconductor Technology**, aimed at preparing skilled professionals to support the rapidly growing semiconductor industry.

## Programme Overview

This postgraduate programme combines theoretical foundations with practical training in semiconductor science and engineering. It is designed to meet the increasing global demand for expertise in semiconductor technology, which underpins key developments in:
- Digitalisation
- Electric vehicles
- Artificial intelligence
- Other emerging technologies

## National Alignment

In line with Malaysia's **New Industrial Master Plan (NIMP) 2030**, the programme contributes to national efforts to position the country as a regional centre for semiconductor innovation and talent development.

Graduates of the programme will be equipped to pursue careers in both academia and industry, as researchers, engineers, or educators.

## Curriculum

The curriculum includes courses in:
- Semiconductor physics
- Materials science
- Device fabrication
- Manufacturing processes
- Cleanroom operations

A strong emphasis is placed on analytical thinking, problem-solving, and hands-on experience through laboratory work, collaborative projects, and industry exposure.

## Who Should Apply

The programme is suitable for:
- Graduates in physics, engineering, and related fields
- Professionals seeking to build or transition their careers into the semiconductor sector
- Those considering doctoral studies or leadership roles in research and development

## Intake and Applications

The first intake is scheduled to begin on **29 September 2025**. Applications are now open.

For more information on admission requirements, course content, and available scholarships, please visit [www.xmu.edu.my](https://www.xmu.edu.my).
